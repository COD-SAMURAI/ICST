Conference Themes

Track 1: Sustainable Energy & Mobility

  • Sustainable/Green Energy
  • Renewable Energy Technologies
  • Energy systems modelling
  • Emerging vehicle Technologies
  • Artificial intelligence and big data for energy transition
  • Green hydrogen and Clean Fuels
  • Micro grids
  • Fuel cells, Batteries, Super capacitors

Handled By :

Dr. Shamsundar S and Dr. Jayasankar V. N.

  • Renewable Energy: Technologies like solar, wind, hydroelectric, and geothermal power contribute to sustainable energy solutions by reducing reliance on fossil fuels.
  • Electric Vehicles (EVs): EVs help reduce greenhouse gas emissions and dependence on oil, promoting sustainable mobility.
  • Smart Grids: These modernize electricity systems to improve efficiency and integrate renewable energy sources effectively.

Track 2: Automation & Robotics

  • Design and Analysis of Robotic systems
  • Sensors for Robotics
  • Special purpose Robots
  • UAV and Drone Technology
  • Semiconductor and Optoelectronic devices

Handled By :

Dr. K R Prakash and Dr. Rajalekshmi Kishore

  • Industrial Automation: Reduces energy consumption and waste in manufacturing processes through optimized operations.
  • Agricultural Robotics: Helps in precision farming, reducing chemical use and optimizing resource efficiency.
  • Autonomous Vehicles: Improves transportation efficiency and safety while potentially reducing traffic and emissions.

Track 3: Materials & Design for Sustainability

  • Smart and energy harvesting materials and devices
  • Soft matter (Polymers, Biomaterials)
  • Nano and Bio-Materials
  • Surfaces, interfaces and thin films
  • AI and ML applications for materials science & Design
  • Design and Analysis
  • Sustainable design technology

Handled By :

Dr. Imran Jamadar and Dr. N C Balaji

  • Biodegradable Materials: Such as bioplastics that reduce environmental impact compared to traditional plastics.
  • Recycled Materials: Using recycled content in manufacturing to conserve resources and reduce waste.
  • Design for Disassembly: Creating products that are easy to disassemble and recycle at the end of their life cycle.

  • Computational modelling

  • IoT for Healthcare and Industries
  • Augmented and Virtual Reality

Track 5: Environment & Green Buildings

  • Sustainable and Green Buildings
  • Air Pollution Control and Air Quality Management
  • Sustainable Water Management Strategies
  • Applications of AI/ML in mitigating climate change issues related to energy-efficient buildings

Handled By :

Dr. K Gourav and Dr. Vasanth Keshav

  • Green Building Design: Uses sustainable materials, energy-efficient systems, and smart technologies to minimize environmental impact.
