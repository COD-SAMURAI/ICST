OUR VISION

NIE is a globally acknowledged institution providing value based technical scientific education through best-in-class talent.

OUR MISSION

  • Impart state-of-the-art engineering education through strong theoretical foundation and practical training to students in their choice of specialization.
  • Create new knowledge through innovation and cutting-edge research in science and engineering.
  • Provide a platform for inclusiveness and collaboration by following ethical and responsible engineering practices for long-term interaction with academia and industry.
  • Encourage entrepreneurship and develop sustainable technologies for the benefit of global society.
