Call For Papers

The conference does not accept or encourage the submission of Survey or Review articles. We aim to maintain a focus on high-quality, original research that contributes new insights and advances in the field of Sustainable Technology.

Submission Guidelines

Prospective authors are encouraged to submit quality and original research papers for presentation at the Conference. Authors are requested to submit their manuscripts in Word/PDF format as per the guidelines of Journal of Institution of Engineers (India) Series A-D through online submission at Microsoft CMT (https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/User/Login?ReturnUrl=%2FICST2025>) - on or before 15th December 2024. The manuscript should follow the standard IEI template (https://www.ieindia.org/webui/iei-home.aspx). All submissions for the conference will be handled electronically on Microsoft CMT platform. The submitted papers will be peer-reviewed by domain experts. The significant contributions from the conference will be communicated to IEI Journals. All the remaining accepted & presented papers in the conference will be communicated to Scopus indexed Journals/Conference Proceedings/Book Chapter, the details about which will be updated on the conference website.
