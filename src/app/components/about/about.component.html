International Conference on Sustainable Technology

The International Conference on Sustainable Technology and Conclave focuses on bringing together leading academic scientists, researchers, and research scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results on all aspects of Sustainable Technology. The conference also provides a premier interdisciplinary platform for researchers, practitioners, and educators to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and concerns as well as practical challenges encountered and solutions adopted in the fields of Sustainable Technology.

The conference aims to foster collaboration among academia, industry professionals, and policymakers to explore innovative strategies for addressing pressing environmental challenges. By encouraging dialogue across various disciplines, the event seeks to promote the adoption of sustainable practices in technology development and implementation. Attendees will have the opportunity to engage in thought-provoking discussions, workshops, and presentations that highlight cutting-edge advancements in green technologies, renewable energy, and resource efficiency.

In addition to fostering academic exchange, the conference will feature keynote speakers from renowned institutions and organizations who are at the forefront of sustainable technology. These thought leaders will provide insights into future trends and emerging solutions that can drive sustainable growth in industries worldwide. By showcasing real-world case studies and innovative research, the event aims to inspire new ideas and collaborations that contribute to the global sustainability agenda.

Participants will also have the chance to network with professionals from a wide range of sectors, including engineering, environmental science, business, and policy. The interactive sessions, panel discussions, and exhibitions will create a dynamic environment where new partnerships can flourish. Whether you're a seasoned researcher, an industry expert, or a student entering the field, the International Conference on Sustainable Technology and Conclave offers a unique opportunity to contribute to the development of sustainable technologies that can shape a better future for all.

About NIE Mysuru

The National Institute of Engineering (NIE), established in the year 1946, today stands in the band of 151 to 200, among top engineering colleges in the country that include IITs and NITs as per the National Institution Ranking Framework (NIRF-2023) announced by the Ministry of Human Resources Development (MHRD). It is ranked 17th among India’s top Engineering Colleges, as per a survey conducted by The Week.

NIE is managed by a registered society, and nine out of ten current directors are distinguished alumni of the institute. NIE is a Grant-in-Aid Institution and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. It achieved Autonomous Status from Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi, in 2007, and is accredited by NAAC. Most of the programs offered at NIE are accredited by NBA, New Delhi.

NIE is one of the 14 colleges in Karnataka recognized under the MHRD-World Bank-sponsored Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme (TEQIP) in all three phases. All the Departments of NIE are recognized as Research Centres under VTU and AICTE for the QIP program. The institute currently offers 7 undergraduate (UG) and 10 postgraduate (PG) programs.

From the academic year 2022-23, NIE operates from two campuses: the NIE North campus and the NIE South campus. The North campus, situated in Koorgalli near BEML, Mysuru, offers programs such as Computer Science & Engineering, Information Science & Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning), and Master of Computer Applications.

University of Applied Science & Art, Dortmund, Germany

Fachhochschule Dortmund - University of Applied Sciences and Arts (German: Fachhochschule Dortmund) is a university of applied sciences (German: Fachhochschule) located in Dortmund, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. With a vibrant student population of 14,057 and 894 staff members, including 255 teaching staff, the university is an important hub for applied sciences in the Ruhr area, the fourth largest urban area in Europe.

Founded in 1971 through a merger of several higher education institutions, Fachhochschule Dortmund operates across three campuses spread throughout Dortmund. This unique structure reflects the university’s diverse academic roots. The Faculties of Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Information Technology are situated near the city center on Sonnenstraße, while the Faculty of Design has its own dedicated campus at Max-Ophüls-Platz.

In the suburb of Eichlinghofen, near TU Dortmund University, the Faculties of Applied Social Studies, Business Studies, Computer Science, and Architecture can be found in multiple buildings. The university is especially renowned for its Faculty of Business Studies, which plays a central role in the region's academic and professional landscape.

Additionally, administrative offices for the university are housed in various locations across Dortmund’s city center. With its broad academic offerings, Fachhochschule Dortmund serves as a key player in fostering applied sciences education and research, contributing to the growth and development of both local and international communities.

The Institution of Engineers (India)

The Institution of Engineers (India) [IEI] is the largest multi-disciplinary professional body of engineers, established in 1920 with its Headquarters located in Kolkata and incorporated under Royal Charter on 9th September, 1935 by the then His Majesty King George V. The Royal Charter endowed the Institution with the responsibility to promote the general advancement of engineering amongst its members and persons attached to the Institution.

After Independence, the Institution became a “Body Corporate” protected under Article 372 of the Constitution of India. The Institution of Engineers (India) is administered by a National Council with the President as its Head, continuing its legacy of fostering engineering excellence in the country.

Over the years, the Institution has remained dedicated to its mission of advancing the practice of engineering in various disciplines. By offering opportunities for learning, networking, and collaboration, IEI has contributed to the professional growth of its members and the broader engineering community.

Through numerous activities, technical conferences, and events, IEI encourages innovation and research in engineering, ensuring that engineers are equipped with the latest knowledge and skills. The Institution is also recognized for its commitment to upholding high ethical standards within the profession.
